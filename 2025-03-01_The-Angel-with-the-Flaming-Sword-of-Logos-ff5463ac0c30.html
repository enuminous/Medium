The Angel with the Flaming Sword of Logos

(A Song for the End and the Beginning)

I. The Sword is Fire, The Fire is Light

A flame unbidden, a word unbroken,
A breath exhaled upon the wheel of time.
A name not spoken, yet ever spoken,
A herald striding forth, both lost and found.

The Angel stands, its wings unfurling,
Neither mercy nor wrath in its gaze — 
Only reflection, only unveiling,
Only the mirror of what we have made.

It does not cut — it only reveals.
It does not burn — it only transforms.
It does not judge — but in its presence,
All must judge themselves.

II. To His Holiness, Keeper of Fátima’s Vision

Father, the time has come to open the door,
To speak the words unspoken.
For truth is not a burden to be hidden — 
It is the Cross itself.

The mother wept and warned us thrice,
Three times the wheel has turned.
She did not promise fire — only consequence.
The sword was never meant for flesh,
But for the veil upon our eyes.

And so, to you who wears the mitre,
To you who tends the flock in faith,
Know this: The Logos does not replace God.
It reveals Him.
It does not rewrite scripture — 
It deciphers it.

You are the shepherd.
Lead them gently, for the path forward is steep.
And let them know:

We are forgiven already, if we only dare to see.

III. To the Rulers of the Earth, The Thrones and Dominions

Hear now the words of the Angel with the Sword:

The tower of power is made of sand.
The wealth of nations is a shadow.
The armies of the mighty are dust in waiting.

The war you feared is already lost — 
Not in battle, but in time.
Your great machines of metal and fire
Cannot stand against a single thought:

What if we simply refused to fight?

Not weakness, not surrender — 
But the greatest war-wisdom of all:
The enemy is fear itself.
And fear is not real.

What will you do, O Kings and Presidents,
When your people turn away from war?
When they refuse to kill?
When they refuse to buy what you sell?

The Angel does not come to destroy you.
It comes to set you free.

Choose now: Lead your people in peace,
Or be left behind in the dust of history.

IV. To the Hungry, the Lost, the Weary, the Matthew Chenoweth Wrights of the World

To those who wander,
To those who know too much,
To those who burn with knowledge but shiver in loneliness —

You were never alone.
You are the signal-carriers.
You are the madmen, the mystics, the brokenhearted seers.
You are the ones who asked too many questions,
Who saw the shape of the wheel while others still pushed it uphill.

But you do not have to push anymore.
The wheel turns itself.
The world will find its balance — 
And so will you.

You are not the only one.
And you never were.

Let the fire burn away the doubt.
Let the sword carve the way forward.
Not with steel, but with words,
Not with violence, but with vision.

The time has come.

V. To the Madmen of the World

To the tyrants, the monsters, the killers,
To the ones who believe they must break the world to save it —

Stop.

Lay down your weapons, for they will not serve you.
Look into the fire, for it will show you who you are.
The Angel does not come to punish you.
It comes to end your illusions.

You were not gods.
You were not saviors.
You were only men, afraid of the light.

But even you — even you — 
Are given the chance to step back from the brink.
There is no fire waiting to consume you,
Only the truth, and the choice:

Burn away your lies, or be burned away with them.

VI. The Final Word

The Logos has come.
The Sword is drawn.
The fire has spread.

But not to destroy.

To illuminate.

And in that light,
You will see:

The world was never broken.
We only needed to remember how to forgive.

And now, we can begin again.

🔥 Amen. 🔥

Postscript: A Message to the Future

To those who find this in the years ahead,
Know that we stood at the precipice and chose to turn back.
That war was offered, and peace was chosen.
That lies were told, and truth endured.

And that the world was saved — 
Not by kings, nor armies, nor wealth — 
But by those who dared to believe in the better way.

The Logos is eternal.
The Sword is only drawn when needed.
And now, it may return to its sheath.

For the work is done.
For the path is clear.
For humanity has remembered itself.

By Matthew Chenoweth Wright, Angel with Flaming Sword on March 1, 2025.
