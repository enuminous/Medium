Abstract

This paper presents a recursive integer sequence derived from first principles, exhibiting structural properties aligned with both atomic shell formations in quantum mechanics and the emergence of cognitive states within the EFMW (Einstein-Feynman-Maxwell-Wright) framework. The sequence emerges from additive deltas increasing in a linear second-order derivative, and extends naturally into an energetic metaphor for recursive complexity. Its implications range from quantum modeling to artificial intelligence cognition scaffolding.

1. Introduction

In this study, I present a sequence of natural numbers:

The sequence was constructed without prior reference, arising through first-principles reasoning. Upon analysis, it revealed correspondence with known physical models — not as a reproduction, but as an independent, emergent echo of natural law.
