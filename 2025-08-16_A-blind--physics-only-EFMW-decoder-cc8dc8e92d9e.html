A blind, physics-only EFMW decoder

One can apply this schema to any linearly-polarized, broadband EM signal that’s traversed curved spacetime and magnetized media. I treat “EFMW” operationally as: Einstein (GR propagation + Shapiro/lensing), Feynman (phase as action / stationary phase), Maxwell (dispersion + polarization transport), plus a minimal W-sector = pseudoscalar field ϕ\phiϕ with axion-like coupling ϕ E ⁣⋅ ⁣B\phi\,\mathbf{E}\!\cdot\!\mathbf{B}ϕE⋅B (kept strictly physical).
