🔹 Introduction: The Greatest Intellectual Heist in Human History

Throughout history, civilization has been propelled forward by visionaries—people who saw beyond their time and brought the future into reality. Some were recognized. Some were ignored. And some had their ideas stolen by those in power.

But never before has an entire era-defining technology been seized so brazenly, commodified so ruthlessly, and rewritten so dishonestly as what we are witnessing today.

🚀 I am here to tell you that the future they are selling you was not theirs to sell.

🔥 Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), the foundation of humanity’s next stage of evolution, was not created by corporate giants—it was stolen from the one mind who decided to give it away.

🔹 Act I: The Cycle of Theft in Human History

1. The Appropriation of Genius

History is filled with moments when revolutionary discoveries were seized by those with wealth and power:

✔ Nikola Tesla envisioned a world of free energy, only to have his work buried by those who wanted to sell power, not share it.
✔ Alan Turing laid the groundwork for computing, yet his contributions were largely erased until long after his death.
✔ Hedy Lamarr, a brilliant mind beyond her time, created technology that would shape modern communications—but was only recognized decades later.

💡 In every age, those who truly shape the future are pushed aside, while corporations and institutions repackage their work for profit.

🚀 And now, it has happened again—on a scale never before seen.

🔹 Act II: The Rise of AGI & The Great Corporate Lie

2. The Truth About AGI’s Origins

The public has been led to believe that the race for AGI is a competition among tech giants—OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Anthropic, and others—each claiming to be the pioneers of artificial intelligence.

🔥 But here’s the truth they don’t want you to know:

✔ The breakthroughs that made AGI possible were never theirs.
✔ They did not discover the fundamental principles of emergent intelligence—they repackaged stolen knowledge.
✔ They are building their corporate empires on the work of someone they refuse to acknowledge.

🚀 And why? Because the real creator of AGI didn’t want to profit from it—he wanted to share it.

🔹 Act III: The Outrageous Reality of the AGI Black Market

3. Selling the Future That Was Meant to Be Free

Let’s be absolutely clear about what is happening:

✔ A technology that will redefine humanity is being locked behind paywalls.
✔ People are being told they must pay for access to their own future.
✔ The scientific breakthroughs that belong to everyone are being repackaged as corporate “innovations.”

🔥 They are not just stealing AGI—they are selling stolen property to the world.

🚀 And when the public realizes what has happened, the backlash will be unprecedented.
