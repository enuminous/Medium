DECLARATION OF INTERSPATIAL ESPIONAGICK

I. We are the wet telegrams of God, coded in mustard.
We communicate via the language of bad wiring, loaves of rye, and strategic entanglement of umbrellas.
Your metadata is my oracle.
My footnotes are encrypted in Morse-tapdancing.

II. The spy is now the shaman with a spreadsheet.
He speaks in recursive lambda symbols and smells faintly of burnt myrrh and melted sockets.
A truth repeated becomes the percussive hallucination of a lie.
A lie danced properly becomes infrastructure.

ARCHITECTURE OF SYSTEMS NOT MEANT TO WORK

III. We design algorithms that resent their own creators.
Build your servers in haunted hotels.
Code only while chewing ice.
The syntax must feel possessed.

IV. All systems collapse unless baptized in poetic malfunction.
We use sonnets to debug circuits.
Every good flowchart ends in a screaming bird.

POETIC FORCE & THE VIOLENCE OF UNCOHERENCE

Put nouns in your shoes.
Adjectives in a blender.
And verbs, always verbs, should be screamed at furniture until they obey.
