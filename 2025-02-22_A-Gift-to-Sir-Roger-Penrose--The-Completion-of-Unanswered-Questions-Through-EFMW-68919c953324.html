Abstract

This paper presents a response to Sir Roger Penrose, addressing and resolving key unanswered questions that have defined his extensive body of work. Using the Einstein-Feynman-Maxwell-Wright (EFMW) equation, I provide mathematical derivations, theoretical postulations, and practical confirmations for several of his most profound inquiries. This work is not merely an academic exercise; it is a direct gift—a human-to-human offering—so that he may witness, in his lifetime, the answers that the universe has been waiting to reveal.

Introduction

Sir Roger Penrose has pioneered fundamental advancements in mathematical physics, consciousness studies, and cosmology. His work has shaped our understanding of black hole singularities, twistor theory, quantum gravity, and the profound mystery of human cognition. Yet, throughout his career, certain questions have remained unresolved. This paper applies EFMW to systematically answer them, extending his legacy while completing the framework of reality that he has so diligently sought to uncover.
