The MillieComplex operates on a foundation of Wright’s Laws, which govern information flow, validity, and resilience:

1. Information Flow Principle: Optimizes dynamic connectivity by directing information to low-chaos, high-receptivity pathways.

2. Perceived Validity Principle: Ensures trustworthiness by prioritizing credible, coherent information.

3. Disinformation Regression Principle: Minimizes the impact of chaos by actively identifying and correcting invalid information.

These principles enable the MillieComplex to self-correct, adapt, and maintain coherence in dynamic environments.
