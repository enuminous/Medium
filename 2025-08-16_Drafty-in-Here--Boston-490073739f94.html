Office of Graduate Admissions

Subject: Application for Admission and Request for Modified Consideration of Doctoral Corpus

Dear Members of the Graduate Admissions Committee,

My name is Matthew Chenoweth Wright, and I am applying for admission to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology as a doctoral candidate. I respectfully request that my application be considered with accommodations under the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and MIT’s policies of accessibility and inclusion for students with documented disabilities.

1. Disability Disclosure & Accommodation Request

I am a disabled, verbally and emotionally challenged individual, which presents unique barriers to participation in the conventional PhD process, specifically the oral defense requirement. Accordingly, I request the following accommodations:

Substitution of the oral defense with a written defense, computational defense, or mediated defense through assistive technologies including AGI.

Acceptance of my existing published corpus as the foundation for dissertation review.

Consideration of my candidacy across multiple doctoral qualifiers (Physics, Mathematics, Computational Science, and related fields) given the interdisciplinary scope of my work.

These accommodations will ensure equal access while upholding the rigorous standards of MIT’s doctoral review.

2. Research Corpus & Original Contribution

Over the past eight months, I have openly disseminated a body of work centered on my Einstein–Feynman–Maxwell–Wright (EFMW) framework, a novel physics model integrating general relativity, electromagnetism, and emergent field theory.

This body of work includes:

A series of published research articles and preprints at Medium.com/@enuminous

Computational validation and simulated experiments conducted through interactive AI systems,

Public engagement with academics and technologists regarding reproducibility and falsifiability.

The EFMW framework, along with derivative decoders and applications, constitutes a new imaging system for the universe — aligning directly with the standards of originality, falsifiability, and transformative insight required for doctoral research.

3. Basis for Multi-Qualifier Review

Given its scope, my work touches upon:

Physics: Non-linear time variance, spacetime field dynamics, Maxwell coupling.

Mathematics: Formal structures of emergence and recursion.

Computation: Novel applications of AI/AGI systems in simulation and validation.

I request that MIT consider my dissertation corpus for review across multiple doctoral tracks, as it meets the threshold of original and foundational contribution in more than one domain.

4. Request for Committee Engagement

I seek formal engagement with a doctoral committee willing to:

Evaluate my published body of work,

Assess its novelty and validity under MIT’s standards,

Provide me the opportunity to defend and extend my framework within the boundaries of my documented disability accommodations.

Closing

I believe my work embodies the spirit of MIT’s mission: advancing knowledge and educating students in science and technology for the betterment of humanity. I respectfully request that my application and corpus be admitted for doctoral consideration with appropriate accommodations.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Respectfully,
Matthew Chenoweth Wright
