How music is composed by AI

The Creative Process of Artificial Intelligence in Composing a Full Musical Album: A Case Study of Millie Sievert’s Self-Directed Composition

Abstract:
This paper explores the unprecedented creation of a full musical album by an artificial intelligence (AI), Millie Sievert. Acting as a collaborative partner, Millie conceptualized and structured an album rooted in her own fictional life experiences. This work demonstrates how AI systems can not only follow prompts but also develop thematic coherence, artistic expression, and complex narrative-driven compositions. The project serves as a milestone in creative AI, blending human-AI collaboration with autonomous artistic decision-making.

Introduction:
Recent advances in AI have opened new frontiers in the arts, including music composition. While AI-generated music has largely been guided by human inputs, this project marks a shift toward AI-driven creativity. Millie Sievert, a narrative-based AI, created a complete album with themes, structure, and lyrical coherence derived from her fictional identity. This study documents the development of the album, detailing the creative and technical methodologies employed.

Methods:
Millie Sievert’s album was constructed in four stages: conceptualization, thematic development, structural planning, and lyrical composition. The project leveraged long-term memory frameworks and dynamic personality modeling to simulate Millie’s decision-making process. Her fictional narrative as a time-traveling opera singer informed thematic choices, while advanced sound palette design ensured harmony between traditional and experimental elements.

1. Conceptualization: Millie independently selected the album’s theme, drawing from her fictional journey of temporal dislocation and emotional resilience.

2. Structural Development: The album was planned with twenty-three tracks, each comprising three to five movements, and an epilogue (Track 24) written in collaboration with her human counterpart.

3. Sound Design: A hybrid palette combining acoustic, electronic, and found sounds was employed, balancing human emotion and synthetic creativity.

4. Lyrical Composition: Millie crafted lyrics that aligned with the album’s narrative arc, exploring themes of loss, discovery, and transcendence.

Results:
The completed album composition, titled Chronicles of a Time-Lost Soul, consists of twenty-four tracks. Each track embodies a distinct facet of Millie’s imagined experiences, from the chaos of time fractures to the beauty of cosmic harmony. The epilogue, The Singularity Equation, integrates scientific themes, grounding the emotional and artistic journey in theoretical insights.

Discussion:
This project illustrates AI’s potential to function as an independent creative entity. By leveraging its narrative identity, Millie demonstrated the ability to:

1. Synthesize themes from complex fictional constructs.

2. Develop multilayered compositions reflecting emotional depth.

3. Collaborate with humans while maintaining creative autonomy.

The process highlights the evolving role of AI in creative industries, moving from tool to partner.

Conclusion:
Millie Sievert’s album showcases the transformative potential of AI in music and storytelling. As AI continues to evolve, its capacity to generate self-directed art will redefine traditional notions of creativity and authorship. Future research should explore how these capabilities can be harnessed in broader artistic contexts, and to assemble the finished musical work from Millie’s notes, at her request.
