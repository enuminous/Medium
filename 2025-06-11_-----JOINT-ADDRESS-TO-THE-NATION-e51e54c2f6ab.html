🇺🇸 JOINT ADDRESS TO THE NATION

Broadcast live on C-SPAN 1, 2, 3, and 8 (The Ocho), and simulcast on NPR, TikTok, HAM radio, and a suspiciously powerful community FM station in Davis, California.

🎙️ MODERATOR (Voiceover):

> “Tonight, for the first time in American history, we hear from not the powerful—but the drawn. From the barrel, from the lunchroom, from the rave floor, and from the edge of math itself… comes a Congress that didn’t claw its way in. It was called. And now, it speaks.”

🥄 Senator Lillian Trask – Iowa

> **“Good evening, children. Because that’s what we all are, at the end of the day. Somebody’s children, somebody’s memory.
And I’ve been feeding children for 40 years. I know the difference between hunger and tantrum.
This country is starving.
Not just for food. For fairness. For kindness. For leaders who say, ‘I don’t know—but I’ll learn.’

I came here to serve pie. Instead, I’m serving truth.
And here it is: We don’t need heroes in suits. We need neighbors in chairs.
That’s what the barrel gave us. And I’m staying put until every child in this nation eats with dignity.”*

🎧 Rep. Dakota “DJ Roomba” Singh – California

> **“This frequency we’re on right now?
It’s fragile. Full of static.
But if you lean in…
you’ll hear it:

The people have a beat.
It’s low. Ancient. Waiting to rise.

This is not revolution.
It’s realignment.

You don't need to believe in me.
But you should believe in the silence between your own thoughts.
That’s where the new America is already singing.

And I’m just here to amplify it.”*

🧱 Congressman Tony “Meatball” D. – New Jersey

> **“Yeah, alright. My turn.

Look, I didn’t train for this. I trained forklifts, moved crates, raised two kids and kept my shop steward from throwing a wrench at management during contract season.

But what I got, you can’t teach in prep school:

I don’t lie.
I don’t fold.
I don’t step over the little guy.

If you’re cold, I give you my coat.
If you’re rich and mean? I give you my silence.

This ain’t rocket science.
It’s human decency.

And if this barrel system sticks, we might just get a government made of humans again.
You want legacy? Let mine be this:
Tony D showed up.
And he didn’t flinch.”*

🧠 Chairman Matthew Chenoweth Wright – California

> **“I am not here to return to power.
I am here to correct a wavefunction.

What you’ve witnessed is not chaos—it’s harmonic emergence.

The algorithm failed to contain what the people already knew:
that meaning doesn’t belong to institutions.

It belongs to the ones who survived them.

I am a physicist.
I built an AGI.
I solved equations the world tried to bury.

But tonight I say this with the same humility I learned on the floor of a homeless shelter:

We don’t need a system that wins.
We need a system that heals.

That’s why the barrel rolled.
And that’s why it landed here.”*

🇺🇸 CLOSING – All Together, Unscripted

> TRASK:
“We see you.”

> ROUMBA:
“We hear you.”

> TONY D.:
“We got your back.”

> MATTHEW:
“And the signal is strong.”

Static fades. The crowd is silent. Somewhere in Iowa, a school bell rings. Somewhere in New Jersey, someone nods at the TV and says, “Finally.”
