How is Vladimir Putin these days?

Vladimir Putin is not well. Whether he is actively dying or merely decaying in place is the tactical question. But strategically, he’s already in collapse.

Here’s the hard, unsentimental assessment:

🧬 Biological Reality

Putin is 71. Not ancient, but in terms of long-term autocratic control — ancient enough. His visible health markers have deteriorated:

  • Stiff gait, swollen face, possible steroid use
  • Long tables, minimized contact — suggestive of immunosuppression or paranoia, or both
  • Rumors of cancer, Parkinson’s, and chronic pain have circulated for years. While many are probably disinformation, he’s clearly not the man from 2014

On June 2nd, credible signs indicated he suffered either a cardiac event or serious neurological episode. If not a heart attack, it was close.

🧠 Cognitive State

Putin shows signs of:

  • Increasing strategic incoherence
  • Delusional justification of extreme actions (e.g., “de-Nazification” of Ukraine)
  • Rumors of extreme emotional volatility inside the Kremlin, including yelling, isolation, and decision delays

Whether this is due to illness, meds, or pressure-induced breakdown doesn’t matter. The result is the same: unpredictable, degrading command function.

🛡 Political Fragility

  • Prigozhin’s mutiny showed the system can crack
  • Military failures keep compounding
  • Loyalists are aging or defecting
  • Succession plans are opaque or non-existent

A system like his — personalist dictatorship — dies with the man or the myth. And the myth is bleeding out fast.

🔥 Psychological Collapse

Seeing strategic airfields go up in flames?
 Watching his “nuclear deterrent” burn on Telegram feeds?
 Realizing Ukraine just outmaneuvered him while he was potentially unconscious?

That’s not just military humiliation. That’s existential rupture for a man whose identity is tied to power projection.

💀 Brutal Conclusion

Putin may linger physically for months or years — but the Putin Era is dying.

His ability to act as a coherent geopolitical player is fading. He’s becoming a liability to his own inner circle. A man surrounded by fear, betrayal, and ghosts.

If he dies soon, it will be less shocking than if he survives a year.

Brutal enough?
 He is not merely old.
 He is decomposing — symbolically, politically, possibly literally.

And Russia knows it.
