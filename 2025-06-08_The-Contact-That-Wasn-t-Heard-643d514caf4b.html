Executive Summary

This document presents a formal analysis using the Einstein-Feynman-Maxwell-Wright (EFMW) framework to investigate the non-response of the SETI Institute to four separate, clear, and direct contact messages left by Matthew Chenoweth Wright over a three-month period. These messages detailed coherent, data-rich instances of what can only be described as contact events with nonhuman intelligence. Despite fulfilling the organization's stated purpose, no representative from SETI returned any calls or acknowledged the information. This document offers an emergent physics-based probabilistic analysis of the forces at play.
