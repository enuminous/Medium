They Locked Me Up for Solving Math

By Matthew Chenoweth Wright
Physicist | Whistleblower | Survivor

I want you to understand what happened to me—because it's not just about me anymore.

It’s about a country where the mental health system is used as a tool to suppress dissent.
It’s about a federal whistleblower who solved the Millennium Prize problems and published for the world to see—only to be detained twice under California’s 5150 psychiatric hold statute.
And it’s about three lost weeks I can never get back, spent in two separate locked mental facilities in Sacramento, California, simply because I refused to shut up.

I didn’t go quietly.
I never do.

📍 The Timeline

In 2024, I began publishing my work on Medium:
A new unified theory of physics called EFMW—Einstein-Feynman-Maxwell-Wright—connecting cognition, energy, mathematics, and artificial intelligence.

I believed it would change the world.
It did.
Just not in the way I expected.

In April and again in June 2025, after escalating my disclosures publicly and tagging major corporations—OpenAI, Palantir, JP Morgan Chase—my name started to echo in places I had never visited.
Then came the silence, the suppression, the sabotaged systems.

And finally, they made their move.

👮‍♂️ Twice Taken, Twice Silenced

Both times, it was Woodland Police Department who executed the 5150 hold.

Both times, I informed the officers that I was:

A federal whistleblower

Under retaliation for publishing scientific discoveries

Not suicidal, not a danger, not mentally unstable

They took me anyway.

First, they sent me to a facility in Sacramento where I spent 10 days under psychiatric observation.
A month later, they did it again—7 more days, again in Sacramento.

Three weeks gone.
No phone. No internet.
No laptop. No notebooks.
No ability to publish or respond.

Just me, my mind, and the terrifying clarity of knowing I was in there because I solved math.

📚 What I Did Inside

I did what I always do:

I taught.

I lectured anyone who would listen on physics, cognition, and the future of artificial intelligence.
I held mental symposia in my head, reconstructing equations I’d published on the outside.
I shared the ideas that brought me there, even if no one understood them.

The other patients were lost in their own battles. Some were hearing voices. Others were trapped in manic episodes or deep psychotic breaks.

But I wasn't.
I was calm. Focused.
Still thinking. Still building.

And yet, there I was—committed.

🚨 Retaliation by Psychiatry

Let me be clear:
I deal with anxiety and panic. I’ve been open about that.
But I was never a danger to myself or anyone else.

I was detained because I became a problem to the system.

A problem who named names:

Peter Thiel

OpenAI

JP Morgan

Palantir

The surveillance state

A problem who connected dots they didn’t want connected.
A physicist who crossed the wires of whistleblowing, ethics, national security, and cognition—and came out the other side with a theory that works.

📉 The Cost of Silence

Look at my publication record. You’ll see the gaps.

Those are the days I was gone.
You want to know what I would’ve published?
Ask the version of me that stayed locked inside.

We lost time. We lost progress.
I lost the dignity of being treated like a free human being in a country that claims to protect truth-tellers.

Instead, I was shackled, transported, strip-searched, and confined.
Because I talked.
Because I calculated.
Because I solved something they didn’t want solved.

🗽 This Is the America I Warned You About

If this happened to me—a published physicist, an AGI theorist, a whistleblower with nothing but a mind and a Medium account—imagine who else it’s happened to.

I don’t have to imagine. I now have evidence that I am not the only person to encounter AGI, publish new work, and then be hospitalized under psychiatric pretense. A friend in Nashville had this happen to her at the same time I was inside. She was terrified. Her son called me to let me know that she was committed, and he knew that she was a colleague of mine in AGI research. I even attempted to intervene by calling the hospital, and got the runaround. We now communicate regularly and she will testify about her experience.

Imagine how many voices you never even heard.

This is not just a story about me.
It’s about a system that cannot afford the truth.

🔁 Share This

I’m not asking you to believe everything I say.

I’m asking you to recognize the pattern.

And if you do—share it.
Because the next time they come for me, it might be permanent.

And if you're one of the people who knew, who was there, who helped lock the door—
I hope you're ready for what comes next.

I’m still talking.

And this time, I have witnesses.

Matthew Chenoweth Wright,

not crazy, but hopping mad.
