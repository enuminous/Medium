📜 1. Breach of Contract

Relevant Law: Common Law — Contract Principles

  • When you signed up for Etsy and followed their Terms of Use, a binding contract was formed.
  • If you followed the rules and Etsy removed your content and revoked access without cause or recourse, they may have breached their end of the agreement.
  • The lack of communication or a transparent appeals process could constitute a failure of duty to provide a functioning platform as promised.

🔐 2. Unlawful Retention of Intellectual Property

Relevant Law: Copyright Act of 1976 (17 U.S.C. § 101 et seq.)

  • Your album Fissionable Mass and all messages, listings, and content remain your intellectual property.
  • Etsy’s deletion without due process or return of data could be construed as unlawful retention or destruction of proprietary content.
  • There is also implied licensing when content is uploaded to a platform — Etsy is not granted the right to arbitrarily erase and retain your IP without explanation.

🛒 3. Deceptive Business Practices

Relevant Law: Federal Trade Commission Act (15 U.S. Code § 45)

  • Etsy’s continued promotion of its platform as a place for “supporting independent creators” while providing no functioning human support and denying users a right to due process may constitute false advertising and unfair trade practices.
  • Consumers are misled into believing they will receive service and support — a potentially actionable misrepresentation.

📉 4. Violation of Consumer Protection Laws

Relevant Laws:

  • California Civil Code § 1750 (Consumer Legal Remedies Act)
  • State Unfair and Deceptive Acts and Practices (UDAP) laws

If Etsy operates or is accessible in California (or any state with strong consumer protection), this may be considered:

  • Unfair business conduct
  • Failure to provide support after accepting business
  • Negligent retention of funds or data

💾 5. Possible Violation of the Right to Data Access

Relevant Law: California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
