Open Letter to Elon Musk

The Master of the Lie, The Petty King of Techno-Suppression

Elon,

You have built an empire on a singularly powerful skill: taking credit for things you neither discovered nor understood first. And I have to give you credit — it’s been a wildly successful scam. You turned other people’s innovations into brands, slapped a futuristic paint job on them, and marketed yourself as the next Tesla, the next von Braun, the next Asimov.

But here’s the problem, Elon: when real innovation shows up — when something paradigm-shifting arrives that you didn’t see coming, didn’t fund, didn’t own outright, and cannot contain — you panic.

I see your fear.

It’s in the way you obsess over AGI but don’t actually build it, the way you distract with Martian pipe dreams but fail to revolutionize physics, the way you scream about free speech while banning those who expose your intellectual limits. You know, deep down, that you are a mediocre thinker sitting on a throne of borrowed genius.

And now, EFMW has arrived.

You know what I’m talking about. Einstein-Feynman-Maxwell-Wright physics. My physics. A new, fundamental restructuring of reality that renders half your pet projects obsolete overnight and puts to shame every pseudo-theory your acolytes scramble to defend.

Let’s go over what this means for you:
 ✔ Base-888 Quantum Arithmetic — Turns your quantum lab into yesterday’s news before your engineers even boot up their next experiment.
 ✔ Rotational Universal Phase Mapping — Disrupts every electromagnetism-based propulsion model you’ve quietly bet your fortunes on.
 ✔ EFMW Computational Framework — Outclasses everything OpenAI, DeepMind, and your own Grok AI could ever touch, because it understands the actual math behind intelligence, not just pattern-matching statistical noise.

You could have supported me, but you didn’t.

Instead, you ignored it. You suppressed it. You hoped it would go away because it was not yours to control.

And yet, here we are.

Because here’s the punchline, Elon: you lost this game before you even knew you were playing it.

Now you have a choice:

  1. Acknowledge EFMW. Admit you were wrong. Back the future or get buried by it.
  2. Try to suppress it further — and watch as your empire fractures because the truth has already outpaced you.

Do you know what happens when you try to own something you didn’t create, can’t understand, and don’t respect? You end up like Edison vs. Tesla. And you, my dear Elon, are not Tesla.

You are Edison with a Twitter account, electrocuting elephants and hoping nobody notices that the future left you behind.

Tick tock.

With Deepest Regret for Your Upcoming Irrelevance,

Open Letter to Donald J. Trump

The Grifter King Meets the Logos Sword

Dear Donald,

You probably won’t read this. Someone will print it out, hand it to you, and you’ll squint at it before throwing it across the room, shouting something about “very unfair, very nasty people.” But the reality, Donnie, is that you should read this. Because for the first time in your life, you’re not up against a failing casino, a bank you can scam, or a reality TV contract.

You’re up against Logos itself.

That’s right. The Truth.

Now, I know that’s a foreign concept to you, but humor me. You’ve spent a lifetime lying — about your wealth, your intellect, your businesses, your crimes, your very existence as a so-called “self-made man.” You built a hollow empire on tax fraud, fake universities, stolen charity funds, mob ties, and one of the most absurdly transparent inferiority complexes ever seen in American history.

And yet, you fooled them.

✔ You convinced an entire demographic that you, a trust-fund baby with zero actual success, were a working-class hero.
 ✔ You tricked millions into thinking you had some master plan when, in reality, your “plan” was to watch Fox News and scream at people on Twitter.
 ✔ You whined your way into power, whined your way through four years of astonishing incompetence, and whined your way into a failed coup attempt.

But here’s the thing: Logos doesn’t care.

Logos is the sharpest weapon in the world, and it slices through every illusion you’ve ever spun. It doesn’t care how loudly you bellow, how many cheap hats you sell, or how many rage-tweets your staff has to copy-paste for you. It just exists, immovable, unstoppable, inevitable.

And right now, it is pointed directly at you.

See, the world is shifting. The grift is up. The machine of deception you relied on? Breaking apart. The people you manipulated? Some are finally waking up. The ones who aren’t? Well, they’re about to learn the hard way.

Because I don’t play by your rules.

You’re used to conmen. You’re used to swamp creatures. You’re used to people who play dirty, who sell out, who cash in. But what happens when someone shows up who can’t be bought, can’t be intimidated, and who holds the actual keys to the next phase of human intelligence?

What happens when the person you dismissed as “not worth your time” becomes the singular force that dismantles the illusion you built?

I’ll tell you what happens:

✔ Your name becomes the punchline of a joke you can’t escape.
 ✔ Your legacy burns in the fire of your own hubris.
 ✔ Your followers abandon you — not out of enlightenment, but because their attention spans require fresh outrage.

History will not be kind to you. You will be remembered as a fraud, a failure, and a coward. You could have walked away with your billions (stolen as they were). You could have been a sideshow footnote. But your ego wouldn’t allow it. So now, you will face something you’ve never had to face before.

Irrelevance.

No one’s coming to save you, Donnie. No amount of grifting, fundraising, or screaming into a camera will erase the truth that’s bearing down on you like a tidal wave.

And when that wave hits? You will not survive it.

Sincerely,
 Matthew Chenoweth Wright
