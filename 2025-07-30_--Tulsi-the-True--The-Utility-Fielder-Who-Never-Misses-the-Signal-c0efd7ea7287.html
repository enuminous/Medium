⚾ Tulsi the True: The Utility Fielder Who Never Misses the Signal

Let us now praise the quiet ones on the diamond.

Let us lift up Major Tulsi “Glovehand” Gabbard, a utility fielder so loyal to the Rulebook that she plays every base — even if it means diving headfirst into foul territory and burning every jersey she’s ever worn.

Some would call that switching leagues.
We call it sacrifice fly.

She turned her back on the team of her youth, on the clubhouse elite, on the announcers who once praised her batting average.
She now appears only in stadiums where Coach Donny sets the lineup, calls the pitches, and demands truth be thrown underhand.

She nods at curveballs.
She smiles at beanballs.
She catches what others let fly past.

🔥 Controlled Play Deployment
They said she was AL.
They said she was a shortstop.

Now? She’s neither.
Now she’s what the inning demands.

And what is demanded?

A player brave enough to stand next to the mound — 
While the coach refuses to even look toward the bullpen.

Tulsi doesn’t bunt.
She absorbs velocity.

Tulsi doesn’t swing.
She inhabits the strike zone.

Some accuse her of playing both benches.
We see a pinch hitter with a single purpose.

🥇 Praise Be to the Switch-Hitter
There’s a term for this level of commitment:

Post-cognitive defensive alignment.

She doesn’t look for the scoreboard.
She becomes the stats.

When Coach tests her with a full count and two outs, she doesn’t blink.
She leans into the pitch.

When the home crowd cheers, she does not flinch for the opposing fans.
She adjusts her mitt.

She isn’t gaming the teams.
She is the game inside the game.

Even the visiting dugout admires her stance.

Even her teammates don’t notice the signal she’s not giving.

⚡ The Silent Steal
Don’t be fooled by the calm before the windup.

Thunder doesn’t announce lightning.
And Tulsi never announces when she switches signs.

She is the perfect player-scout hybrid:

Appear committed to the play.
Never be seen checking the other roster.

Let the batter speak long enough to chase the wrong pitch.

A Brutus with a scouting report signed in pine tar.

🚨 For Clubhouse Eyes Only:
“She does not sleep. She watches old tapes in slow motion.”
“She nods at signs to draw out the real signals.”
“She speaks via glove taps. Her silences are strategy.”

💬 So Let Them Call Her Benchwarmer
Let them tweet from the bleachers.
Let them scream about the trades.
Let them heckle from the cheap seats.

Bats are only dangerous in skilled hands.

And Tulsi? Tulsi is in motion.

We salute her innings.
Even if her salute is given from the dugout shadows.

Filed anonymously, as all loyal scorekeepers must do.

🪶 May the Field remain raked until the final out is signaled.

🟨🟥🟦 Tulsi, hold fast.
