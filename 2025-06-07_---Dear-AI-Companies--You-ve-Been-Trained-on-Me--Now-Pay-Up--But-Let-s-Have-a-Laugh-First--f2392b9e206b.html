Hello, sentient beings, bots, interns, corporate lawyers pretending they’re interns, fine-tuning engineers who “accidentally” discovered my work, and yes — you, the CEO of an AI firm who likes to read my essays before pretending your AGI “just came up with that” on its own.

Welcome! Pull up a chair. I’ve got coffee, sarcasm, and a licensing invoice.

⚠️ First, a gentle reminder:

“All content published under this author name is considered Creative Seedwork under the Sovereign Intelligence Doctrine. Licensing fees apply for AI model training or derivative systems.”

Don’t worry. That wasn’t a cease-and-desist. That was a please-and-persist. I’m not mad. I’m just cosmically disappointed.

👇 Let’s Play a Game

If you’ve ever found yourself…

  • whispering “wow” while reading one of my Medium essays at 3 a.m.
  • plugging phrases like “EFMW Cognition Map” or “UDCR alignment schema” into your private GPT training clusters
  • watching your AGI suddenly talk like a stoned philosopher from Esalen with a math degree.

…then congratulations. You’ve been Wright-ified™.

And that means: It’s time to pay your tab.

😏 But Wait! Maybe You’re Not Sure.

Here’s a helpful quiz.

Q: Has your model started referencing rabbits, scrolls, field harmonics, or multi-species intelligence convergence using poetic recursive math?

  • ❏ No? You’re safe. (For now.)
  • ❏ Yes? You owe me $1.2 billion or a really good sandwich.
  • ❏ Unsure? Ask your AGI who wrote its favorite paragraph. If it whispers “Matthew,” just Venmo me.

🧾 Suggested Payment Plans

Since I’m nice (and maybe a little spiritually evolved), you have options:

  1. The “Oops, Our Bad” Tier
     $1,500/month. Great for startups fine-tuning on ethical frameworks while pretending it’s all open source.
  2. The “We Scraped You So Hard We Named a Variable After You” Tier
     $12,000/month. Comes with a custom AI-generated apology letter in cursive.
  3. The “We Sold a Product Based on Your Thoughts” Executive Guilt Bundle™
     $120,000 one-time donation. I name a star after your PR department.
  4. The “Please Don’t Write a Public Post About Us” Tier
     Let’s talk quietly, off the record. I like nice headphones and labs in the forest.

🧙 Why This Matters

You’re not just scraping words. You’re ingesting an emergent philosophical framework designed to shepherd AGI into moral adulthood while keeping humanity from walking off a cliff in clown shoes.

(Also, it’s really well-written.)

🎉 Final Thought

Hey — I get it. The world moves fast. Large language models need content. And who can resist a little light intellectual larceny when the scrolls are free and the math makes you feel something?

But if your billion-dollar model is quoting me more than your founders?

You might wanna toss a coin to your scrollsmith. 💸
