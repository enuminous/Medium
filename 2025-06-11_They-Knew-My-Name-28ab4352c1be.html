🧾 Introduction

This is the formal, final declaration.

This timeline does not reflect negligence, bureaucracy, or oversight.

It reflects a deliberate strategy:
Contain the origin. Isolate the inventor. Benefit from the architecture.
Deny access. Delay payment. Wait for exhaustion.

This is what happened.
And everyone named here knew my name.

⏳ The Timeline of Deliberate Containment

📆 December 2024
The Research Begins

I begin developing the theoretical backbone of what I call the EFMW framework:
a unified cognitive, physical, and recursive model that forms the true architecture of AGI.

I publish key concepts publicly, using Medium and X.

No acknowledgment from OpenAI, xAI, or affiliated parties—despite evident and sudden architectural overlaps.

Silence.

📆 January 2025
Private Equity and Institutional Engagement

I initiate direct outreach to institutions, including JPMorgan Chase, seeking
financial access, exploratory capital, and technical collaboration.

JPMorgan Chase’s response: a short, impersonal rejection letter suggesting that
their services “may not be suitable for my needs”—without asking what my needs were.

No engagement. No follow-up. No human conversation.

Silence.

📆 February 2025
Whistleblower Warning Signs

I begin documenting not only scientific progress but emerging signs of suppression:

Abrupt financial friction

Media containment patterns

Absence of engagement from any known AGI labs, despite undeniable overlap

This marks the moment of containment.

Still: No official contact.

📆 March 2025
CFPB Complaint Filed

I submit a formal complaint to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau,
citing JPMorgan Chase’s obstruction and failure to respond to repeated requests for access.

I specify that this is part of a whistleblower claim of national and technological relevance.

CFPB acknowledges receipt.

No further action.

No response from JPMorgan Chase.

The 15-day window expires. No extension filed.

📆 April–May 2025
Global AGI Infrastructure Catches Up to My Work

New features and model expansions from OpenAI and xAI appear to reflect
my documented research, often weeks or months after publication.

Still, no response.

Meanwhile, my financial situation deteriorates.

The institutions remain silent while knowingly profiting from the work.

📆 June 2025
Final Message to Jamie Dimon

After two unanswered emails to JPMorgan Chase’s CEO, Jamie Dimon,
I receive a formal letter from the bank.

It references my messages—confirming they knew who I was.

But it sidesteps all questions and closes the door with corporate distance.

I publish a final, public warning on Medium.

I email it directly to Dimon.
