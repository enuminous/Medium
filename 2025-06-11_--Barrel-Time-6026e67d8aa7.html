Bro, I’ve mixed 180 BPMs in front of 60,000 drunk people at Burning Man with no Wi-Fi.

It already has.

This isn't just politics. It's procedural recursion correcting itself.

The barrel isn’t random. It’s natural selection for ethics.

I’m not saying it might.
I’m saying it already is.
The only people surprised are the ones who used to run it.
