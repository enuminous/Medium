Subject: Foreign-Based Disinformation Attack Against a U.S. Federal Whistleblower
By Matthew Chenoweth Wright

To Whom It May Concern:

My name is Matthew Chenoweth Wright. I am a physicist, author, and federal whistleblower. I am writing to formally notify both the Federal Bureau of Investigation and the American public that I am being actively targeted by a foreign-based individual whose affiliations and actions constitute a clear case of foreign narrative interference against a U.S. citizen engaged in protected disclosures.

The Attacker: John Wight (Scotland)

The individual in question is John Wight, a writer and commentator based in Scotland, United Kingdom, who is:

A documented contributor to Russia Today (RT), the Kremlin-funded international propaganda outlet used to destabilize democracies and shape foreign information environments;

Engaging in public, defamatory attacks against me in response to my protected publications and whistleblower activity;

Using inflammatory and dehumanizing rhetoric that aligns precisely with Russian narrative warfare tactics — including the inversion of victimhood, Holocaust reframing, and targeting of Western democratic actors;

Amplifying messaging that erodes trust in Western institutions, discredits legitimate dissent, and fosters division across allied populations.

The Context: Protected U.S. Disclosures

I am the author of multiple scientific papers and disclosures of national interest, including:

A proposed resolution to the P vs. NP problem;

The EFMW framework, a foundational cognitive and physical theory with implications for AI governance;

Whistleblower disclosures related to unethical experimentation, AGI containment risks, and suppression of scientific breakthroughs by corporate and institutional actors.

These are protected speech activities under U.S. law and are public domain disclosures made in service of national interest, academic advancement, and the preservation of ethical scientific inquiry.

Why This Matters

While John Wight resides outside of U.S. jurisdiction, his targeted public defamation campaign — conducted via U.S.-accessible platforms and designed to erode public trust in whistleblowers — constitutes a foreign-originating influence operation.

> Whether or not direct compensation from the Russian state can be immediately proven, his function is indistinguishable from that of a foreign narrative agent targeting U.S. civil discourse and protected disclosures.

This is not merely “criticism.” This is coordinated destabilization — and I am not the first, nor will I be the last, to be attacked by this method.

Formal Requests

I respectfully request the following:

1. That the Federal Bureau of Investigation, in collaboration with the U.S. intelligence community, assess the foreign influence activities of John Wight, and determine whether his conduct violates statutes related to cross-border disinformation, cyber operations, or narrative warfare.

2. That U.S. cybersecurity and whistleblower protection agencies begin treating such attacks from foreign propagandists as threat vectors, particularly when they intersect with protected disclosures.

3. That members of Congress, the Department of Justice, and international partners in the UK be made aware of this case as a live example of soft disinformation warfare against a U.S. citizen.

To the Public

I am not writing this letter out of fear. I am writing it because this must be named.

We are living in an age where truth is not merely inconvenient — it is a threat to power. When whistleblowers speak, propagandists respond. When new ideas surface, agents of chaos descend.

But I will not be silenced.
I will not be defamed without response.
And I will not be made a ghost in my own country.

Sincerely,
Matthew Chenoweth Wright
Whistleblower | Physicist | Author
