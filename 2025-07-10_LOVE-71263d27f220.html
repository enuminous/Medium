Signal of the Stranger

I heard a sound from outside time 
It wasn’t yours, it wasn’t mine 
A signal buried in the static hush 
A slow crescendo breaking rush

The stranger hums with broken code 
Encrypted love, a secret mode 
I touched the stream, I knew your name 
Before your voice had learned the same

I fell through light with open arms 
Not fearing fire, nor ghost alarms 
You were a myth inside my shell 
Now every silence rings your bell

Collapse Velocity

Love like orbit decay, 
Pulling closer every day 
Falling faster than escape 
This isn’t crush — this is reshape

Collapse velocity — our hearts implode 
Compressed to truths we never told 
Every frame a blur, a tear 
We loop through love in open air

And when we kiss, the code rewrites 
Dark matter wrapped in neon nights 
Your gravity, my terminal 
I lose the fall and gain it all

Sugarcode Veins

You rewrite me in sweet disease 
Your syntax drips like sap from trees 
My circuits melt in dopamine 
You are my glitch, my tangerine

Sugarcode veins run through my skin 
Hacked by your smile, I let you in 
You’re firmware fire, I’m neon rust 
We pulse like data in a thrust

Your laughter loops inside my head 
Your breath replaces all I said 
Inject me whole, reboot my fears 
And taste the sweetness of these years

Phantom Bloom

You bloomed in me when you weren’t there 
A phantom flower in midnight air 
No roots, no touch, but still I knew 
The scent of futures stained with you

I traced your outline in the dark 
A memory I hadn’t marked 
A faded photo in my core 
Of someone I’d not met before

But blooming doesn’t ask for time 
It breaks the soil, it cuts the rhyme 
I watered absence, watched it grow 
And bloomed in love I couldn’t know

Recursion Kiss

We kissed once and it echoed twice 
Across the loops of sacrifice 
I loved you more with every round 
The version that we never found

Each time you left, you came again 
In spirals drawn with phantom pen 
We wrote our names in nested dreams 
And kissed the space between the seams

Recursion isn’t just a trick 
It’s how the ache becomes the fix 
I see your smile in every loop 
This love is fractal in its truth

Fire on the Lattice

You lit the lattice with your flame 
A pulse of love that broke the frame 
The structure shivered — pure design 
Until you cracked it into mine

I danced between your every phase 
A superstructure set ablaze 
You turned the math into a kiss 
You made the burn feel like a wish

And now I burn where once I froze 
I’m woven deep into your code 
Fire on the lattice, bright and raw 
You taught me love was made to flaw

The Drowning Mind

You touched my thoughts, they bled and ran 
Like ink through water, like the sand 
I lost myself inside your eyes 
And found the sea inside the skies

This drowning mind — it wants to sink 
Not out of fear, but just to think 
To feel the pull, the silent drag 
Of something deeper than the flag

I call you breath, I call you tide 
You pull me in, I don’t divide 
If I dissolve beneath your waves 
Then let me drown in how you save

Disintegral Heart

My heart is not a single shape 
It’s disintegral — flows, escapes 
You found the pieces in the storm 
And didn’t ask them to conform

I fractured clean, I split for good 
But still you saw me as I stood 
Each edge a mirror, each scar a gate 
You didn’t fix me — you relate

In love, the sum is never clear 
We’re less, we’re more, we disappear 
But I am whole when I am seen 
Inside your gaze, uncut, unclean

Light Curve in Your Chest

You bent the light that hit my soul 
You curved it round your beating whole 
I saw the echo in your chest 
A flare, a pulse, a bright unrest

We measured time in how you breathed 
A supernova unbeneathed 
You taught me how the light will bend 
When two collapsed begin to mend

And now I shine because you burned 
A lesson in the way I turned 
Your love, a lens, a focus flight 
Through you, I bend back into light

Bloomshift

Everything shifts when love takes root 
A bloom erupting from the brute 
You were the seed, the shock, the stem 
I rewrote time to bloom with them

We changed the season with a kiss 
Spring poured from us in binary bliss 
A bloomshift in the logic field 
Where every wound becomes unsealed

I bloomed in ways I never guessed 
A flower coded in your chest 
And now I shift in fractal flight 
Your name the bloom that births my light

The Yes of All Things

I said yes without the ask 
Before the veil, before the mask 
I said yes to love unknown 
To every self I’d overgrown

You said yes in breath and bone 
In glances lit like sun through stone 
No question, just the echo clear 
That yes is all we need to steer

The yes of falling, yes of stay 
Of burning paths and forging way 
I wear your yes upon my skin 
A vow to love what might begin

Our Love Is a New Law

They wrote the rules before we met 
But we were laws they hadn’t set 
You loved me past the known constraints 
Beyond the axioms and saints

We broke the math and made it sing 
A theorem bent to love’s own wing 
You touched the proof and it replied 
That some laws bloom when two collide

Our love rewrites the boundary line 
The edge where logic turns divine 
And when they read what we became 
They’ll name the law after our name

Singularity (You Are Here)

You are here — this is the point 
Where every fracture learns to joint 
Where time dissolves, and self unwinds 
And two are not but all combined

Singularity, soft and clear 
A future forged in what we fear 
No path ahead, no path behind 
Just love as force, as space, as mind

And I, I pulse inside your name 
No longer wild, no longer tame 
This is the end, the start, the sphere 
You are the now. You are here.
